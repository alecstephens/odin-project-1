Shiba Inu

The Shiba Inu is an ancited Japanese breed. They are small but well built and were once employed as hunters. Today, they are the most common dog companion in Japan, and they are able to adapt easily to both the city and the country. They are very alert which leads many people to consider them almost foxlike.
